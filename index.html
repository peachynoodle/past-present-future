Wen's Past, Present, and Future

Past

I was born in Greenville, SC and I've lived here for my entire life. My childhood was filled with going outside to play with my friends and riding bikes, playing pretend, drawing on the walls (which I got in trouble for), and watching cartoon shows that don't air anymore. From then on, elementary and middle school went by terribly fast. I went to Mauldin Elementary and I barely remember anything, but I remember much more from my experience at Beck Academy. I've moved around twice: once when I was very young and another time about two years ago. Below are some highlights from my past:

Highlights From My Past

  1. Received the Mightly Eagle (Highest GPA) Award at Mauldin Elementary
  2. Attended the A.R.M.E.S. Program at the Fine Arts Center
  3. Attended My First Convention
  4. Cut Hair Short After 14 Years of Long Hair
  5. Got Accepted into the Fine Arts Center High School Program

Present

My immediate family consists of myself, my older brother (who is 21 currently), and my mom and dad. I don't get to see my brother as often since he's attending Clemson University. I do get to see my parents a little more, and they both are very hard-working. An example is how we renovated our house together before we moved in. It was a lot of work and my dad did a bulk of it. Our house looks so much better, inside and outside, compared to when we first bought it. I'm glad that it was something we all contributed to. Our family has a great dynamic and we all care and love about each other.

My current interests include drawing, listening to music, dancing, talking to friends, and taking long naps. I have always liked doing a majority of these things, but some have become bigger parts in my daily life. I focus a lot on drawing and art in general, since I want to make this hobby into a career. Overall, my life is actually quite boring when I think about it. Everything is a cycle: go to school, go to the Fine Arts Center, go home, take a nap, draw, eat, do my homework, then rinse and repeat.

Future

My plans after high school are to go to college and get a good job afterwards. I plan to major in either Illustration or Graphic Design (both of which have many jobs available.) I really would like to attend college in New York City. I'm looking at the School of Visual Arts and hope that my portfolio is strong enough for me to get in. I plan on going up to New York City to do a summer program this year and get a feel of the school and its atmosphere. Hopefully, everything will work out well.
